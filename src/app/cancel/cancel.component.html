Cancel and Refund Policy for FoodCourt

Cancellation Policy

Order Cancellation

Customers have the right to cancel their order within a specified time frame. To cancel an order, customers must contact our customer support team via foodcourt2312@gmail.com. within 1 minute of placing the order. Orders cannot be canceled once the food preparation process has begun.

Modification of Orders

Customers may request modifications to their orders, such as changes to delivery address or contact information, within 1 minute of placing the order. After this time frame, modifications cannot be guaranteed.

Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds will be provided in the following situations:

Quality Issues: If the delivered food does not meet our quality standards.

Non-Delivery: If the ordered food is not delivered within the specified time frame.

Order Cancellation: If a valid order cancellation request is made within the allowed time frame.

Refund Process

Quality Issues: If customers are dissatisfied with the quality of the food delivered, they must contact our customer support team within 1 hour of receiving the order. Customers may be required to provide photographic evidence of the quality issue.

Non-Delivery:If the ordered food is not delivered within the specified time frame, customers should contact our customer support team immediately for assistance.

Order Cancellation:Refunds for canceled orders will be processed within 2 business days from the date of cancellation. The refund will be issued using the same payment method used for the original transaction.

Non-Refundable Items

Certain items may be non-refundable. This includes, but is not limited to, delivery charges and taxes.
